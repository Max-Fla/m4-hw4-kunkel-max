About Dua

Dua Lipa is a 26 year-old singer and songwriter who was born in London and is of Kosovo Albanian descent. Lipa signed with Warner Bros. Records in 2014 and released her self-titled album Dua Lipa three years later. The album recieved numerous accolades and has since been certified platinum in several countries.

Dua's second album, Future Nostalgia, was released on March 27th, 2020. The album surpassed the success of her first and launched her into the mainstream. Lipa is currently on the European leg of her world tour and has since launched a weekly newletter and podcast.
